# LongBao Material Guide v1

## Materials

- Dinosaur skin: soft matte, gentle green, subtle texture
- Cat onesie: short plush fabric, warm cream color, visible soft folds
- Backpack: canvas-like light brown material
- Growth journal: kraft paper / notebook leather texture
- Camera: soft toy-safe plastic and dark lens material

## Rule

LongBao should feel soft, warm, and touchable. Avoid glossy toy plastic or harsh realism.
