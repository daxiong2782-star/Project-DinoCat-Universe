# Day001 Video Prompts

## Style

Family-friendly 3D animated short, cinematic camera, smooth character animation, expressive body language, warm lighting, Pixar-quality, cute comedy, travel adventure.

## Shot 01

Camera slowly pushes in as LongBao enters a Tokyo street with a small suitcase, looking up at Tokyo Tower in awe.

## Shot 02

Medium close-up of LongBao inspecting a vending machine, tilting his head like it is a robot.

## Shot 03

Close-up: soda bottle drops out, LongBao grabs it and shakes it excitedly.

## Shot 04

Fast comedic timing: soda sprays upward, LongBao freezes in shock, small cat blinks after being lightly splashed.

## Shot 05

Gentle shot: LongBao bows and wipes the cat with a towel, embarrassed but sincere.

## Shot 06

Sunset shrine path. The cat nudges a glowing rainbow badge toward LongBao. LongBao gasps softly.

## Shot 07

LongBao opens a map, the first badge glows, camera pulls back to show Tokyo evening lights.
