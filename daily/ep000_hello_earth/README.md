# EP000 — 你好，地球。
# Hello, Earth.

## Purpose
EP000 is the official opening trailer for LongBao Growth Journal.

This episode is not designed to explain the full worldview.
Its only goal is to let the audience meet LongBao and feel his curiosity, softness, and emotional promise.

## Core Goal
Make the audience like LongBao within 45 seconds.

## Core Theme
第一次来到地球。

## Growth Anchor
好奇与开始。

## Brand Ending
龙宝打开《成长日记》，写下第一句话：

> 今天，我来到了地球。  
> 原来世界，比我想象中还要大。  
> 我想每天成长一点点。
