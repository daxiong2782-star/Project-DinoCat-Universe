# EP000 Video Prompts

## General Video Style
- Duration: 30–45 seconds
- Aspect ratio: 9:16
- Style: warm 3D cinematic animation
- Motion: slow, gentle, readable
- Dialogue: minimal
- Music: soft piano / warm ambient
- Sound: wind, grass, soft camera click, pencil writing

---

## Shot 01 Video Prompt
A small cute star travel capsule lands softly in a quiet grassy field. The door opens slowly. LongBao, a green baby dinosaur wearing a beige plush cat onesie, peeks out carefully with big curious brown eyes. Camera slowly pushes in. Warm morning light, soft wind, gentle cinematic 3D animation.

Duration: 5 seconds

---

## Shot 02 Video Prompt
A leaf floats down in slow motion and lands in LongBao's small hand. LongBao looks at it closely, smiles softly, and places it into his backpack. Gentle close-up, warm lighting, emotional quiet moment.

Duration: 7 seconds

---

## Shot 03 Video Prompt
LongBao squats down on a grassy path and gently waves to a small cat. The cat walks closer, looks at him, then runs away calmly. LongBao smiles and waves goodbye. No chasing. Soft friendly tone.

Duration: 8 seconds

---

## Shot 04 Video Prompt
LongBao looks up as birds fly across a bright sky. He lifts a small camera and takes his first photo on Earth. Add a soft camera click. Camera angle from below, showing wonder and curiosity.

Duration: 10 seconds

---

## Shot 05 Video Prompt
Evening. LongBao sits beside a warm window, opens his Growth Journal, draws a leaf, a cat, and birds, then writes the first diary sentence. Soft pencil-writing sound, warm sunset glow.

Duration: 10 seconds

## Shot 06 Video Prompt
Close-up of the closed Growth Journal cover. Text appears: “LongBao's Growth Journal”. Then slogan appears: “每天成长一点点。” Fade out gently.
