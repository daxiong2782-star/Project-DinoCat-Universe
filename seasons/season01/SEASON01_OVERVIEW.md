# Season 01 Overview

## Title
Finding the Rainbow Badges

## Core Story
LongBao arrives on Earth and travels across countries and cities to collect Rainbow Badges.

## Season Goal
Establish LongBao as a warm, funny, curious global travel character.

## Structure
- Days 001–030: Arrival and first discoveries
- Days 031–090: Food and culture
- Days 091–180: New friends
- Days 181–270: World wonders
- Days 271–365: Growth and return-home choice

## Emotional Arc
LongBao starts as a lost child and grows into a brave traveler who understands kindness, culture, and friendship.
