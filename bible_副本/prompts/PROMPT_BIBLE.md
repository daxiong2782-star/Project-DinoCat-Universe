LongBao, a cute green baby dinosaur wearing an oversized beige cat onesie, big round eyes, soft fluffy fabric, small travel backpack, tiny camera, warm smile, family-friendly 3D animated character, Pixar-quality, Disney-style charm, DreamWorks expressive body language, cinematic lighting, ultra detailed, global travel adventure, warm color palette, consistent character design.

## 使用规则

每次生成图片或视频提示词时，必须先引用基础角色 Prompt，再添加当天国家、场景、动作、镜头和情绪。
